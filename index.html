Frequently asked questions

What actually happens in a Tech Audit?

We hop on a 60-minute video call and go through whatever's slowing you down, whether that's one tool, a workflow, or your whole setup. You'll leave with a clear picture of what's going on and, where possible, 1 to 2 quick fixes done there and then.

I don't really know what I need, where do I start?

That's completely normal, and exactly what the Tech Audit is for. You don't need to diagnose the problem yourself. Tell me what feels hard or what isn't working, and I'll help you figure out what's actually going on underneath it.

How quickly can I book a Tech Audit?

Booking is instant. Pick a slot that works for you using the booking link, and you'll get a confirmation straight away.

What's the difference between a Tech Audit and a VIP Day?

A Tech Audit is about diagnosis and quick wins. A VIP Half-Day or Full-Day is dedicated execution time, used for building or fixing something properly, like a booking system setup, a full tool migration, or launching an online programme.

Do you work with specific tools or software?

I work across most of the popular small business tools, including booking platforms, email marketing tools like MailerLite, Google Workspace, Zapier automations, and website builders. If you're not sure whether your tool stack is covered, just ask.

Is the Monthly Tech Retainer right for me?

If you've already got your systems set up and just want someone keeping things running, updated, and ticking over, the retainer is built for that. It covers maintenance and small edits rather than new builds or projects.

Do you work with businesses outside your local area?

Yes. Most of my work happens over video call and screen share, so I can support you wherever you're based.

Can we meet in person?

I'm based in Essex, and depending on your location and the package you choose, in-person support can sometimes be arranged. It's always a bespoke discussion between us, and any travel costs are added on top. Just ask if this is something you'd like to explore.
